Privacy Policy for Bill Tracker

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

Bill Tracker ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Bill Tracker uses local SQLite database storage on your device to save your bills, payment history, and app settings. Your data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers or third parties.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Bill Tracker does not:

  • Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Require account creation or login
  • Connect to the internet for data storage or synchronization

Data Types Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Bill names and descriptions
  • Bill amounts and due dates
  • Payment history records
  • App preferences and settings
  • Notification schedules

Permissions

Bill Tracker requests the following permissions:

Notifications

  • Purpose: To send you reminders about upcoming bill payments
  • How it works: Notifications are scheduled locally on your device (3 days before and on the day bills are due)
  • Your control: You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time

Exact Alarms (Android)

  • Purpose: To ensure notifications are delivered at the precise scheduled time
  • How it works: Allows the app to schedule exact alarm times for bill reminders
  • Required for: Reliable notification delivery on Android devices

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is no sharing of information with:

  • Third-party services
  • Analytics providers
  • Advertisers
  • Other applications
  • Cloud storage services

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since data is stored locally:

  • Data is only accessible on your device
  • Data is protected by your device's lock screen and security measures
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or update your bills and payment records at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual bills, payments, or uninstall the app to remove all data

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Delete individual bills or payments from within the app, or
  2. Uninstall the app from your device, which will permanently delete all stored data

There is no server-side data to delete since all information is stored locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Bill Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require personal information from any users, including children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Third-Party Services

Bill Tracker does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.
